### Commands:

- \add {word} - adds word to list
- \addMany {words separated only by a comma} - adds many words to list
- \show - shows the list of words
- \remove {word} - removes the word from list

### Examples:

- \add {word} - \add word
- \addMany {words separated only by a comma} - \addMany word,word2,word3
- \remove {word} - \remove word
